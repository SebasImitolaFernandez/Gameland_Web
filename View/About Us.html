ABOUT US

GameLand was created from a shared passion for video games and the communities that grow around them. For many gamers, playing a game is only part of the experience. Gaming events, conventions, and esports tournaments allow players to meet other fans, discover new games, and celebrate the culture that connects millions of people around the world. However, many gamers struggle to find information about these events or to purchase tickets easily. Important conventions, esports competitions, and gaming festivals are often spread across different platforms, making it difficult for fans to stay informed. GameLand was born to solve this problem. The idea started as a project created by students and developers who wanted to build a platform dedicated to gaming events. The goal was simple: create a website where gamers could discover upcoming events, learn about them, and buy tickets in a simple and accessible way. Inspired by large gaming conventions like G3 and other international gaming festivals, GameLand was designed to bring the gaming community closer to the events that shape the industry. From major eSports tournaments to indie game showcases and community gatherings, the platform aims to highlight the diversity and creativity of the gaming world. As the project developed, GameLand evolved from a simple concept into a growing platform focused on connecting gamers, event organizers, and developers. The website continues to expand with the ambition of becoming a central hub where gamers can explore events, plan their experiences, and be part of the global gaming community.

Our Story

GameLand’s mission is to make it easy for gamers to discover and enjoy video game events around the world. Our platform connects players, event organizers, and developers in one place, allowing users to find conventions, esports tournaments, and gaming festivals and purchase tickets quickly and securely. By simplifying access to these events, we aim to strengthen the global gaming community and help people share their passion for video games.

Mission

Our vision is to become the leading reference platform for video game events in the region, serving as the essential bridge between gamers, emerging talent, and major brands. We aspire to raise the standard of both competitive and recreational gaming entertainment by creating a space where innovation, community, and opportunity come together.

Vision

At GameLand, our work is guided by core values that shape how we build and manage our platform, we follow the TCTPA philosophy:

Values

  • Transparency: We communicate clearly and honestly with our community, partners, and users.
  • Community: We believe gaming should be accessible to everyone and we strive to create welcoming spaces for all players.
  • Trust: We prioritize secure systems and responsible practices so users can enjoy events and services with confidence.
  • Passion: Our commitment to the gaming world drives us to support events, players, and creators.
  • Simplicity: We aim to remove barriers and meet our customers needs and make it easy for them to attend gaming events.
